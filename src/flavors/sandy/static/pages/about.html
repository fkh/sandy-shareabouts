About this map

Opportunities to volunteer with Sandy cleanup are being posted to Twitter with the #SandyVolunteer hashtag.

Post locations mentioned on Twitter to the map, and add comments and clarifications to keep the information up to date. You can filter the view by type of issue. This is a moderated map, so your comments will be used to update the listings at each location.

This map is powered by Shareabouts. Comments, feedback and moderating assistance welcome -- @fkh.
